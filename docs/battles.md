# ⚔️ Battle System Guide

Complete guide to the battle and RPG system.

## Monster Hunting

### `/hunt`
Hunt monsters for rewards.

**Monsters:**
- Slime (Common)
- Goblin (Uncommon)
- Wolf (Rare)
- Orc (Epic)
- Dragon (Legendary)

**Rewards:**
- Coins (based on difficulty)
- XP (based on difficulty)
- Materials (random)

**Cooldown:** 30 seconds

---

## PvP Battles

### `/battle <user> [wager]`
Challenge another user to battle.

**Features:**
- Turn-based combat
- Optional coin wager
- Winner takes all

**Cooldown:** 60 seconds

---

## Pet System

### `/pet view`
View your pets.

### `/pet summon`
Summon a random pet.
- **Cost:** 2000 coins

**Pet Types:**
- Dog (Common)
- Cat (Uncommon)
- Eagle (Rare)
- Lion (Epic)
- Phoenix (Legendary)

---

## Equipment

### `/equip <item>`
Equip weapons or armor.

### `/equipment`
View equipped items.

**Slots:**
- Weapon
- Helmet
- Chest
- Boots

---

## Stats

### `/stats [user]`
View battle stats.

**Displayed:**
- Attack power
- Defense
- Level
- HP
- Pets owned
