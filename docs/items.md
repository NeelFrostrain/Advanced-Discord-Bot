## Crafting

### Commands
- `/craft list` - View recipes
- `/craft recipe <item>` - View recipe
- `/craft make <item>` - Craft item

### Recipes
```
Iron Sword: 5 Iron Ore + 2 Wood
Steel Sword: 10 Iron Ore + 2 Magic Crystal + 3 Wood
Health Potion (x3): 2 Wood
```
